# BlockLands
##### (Using Copywrite Mario assets for the test. This will be deleted soon)

## Controls
- **WASD** or **Arrowkeys**: Move
- **Spacebar**: Jump / Swim up
- **Shift**: Crouch

## Special Moves
- **Flip Jump** - Jump higher than the regular jump. Crouch then Jump
- **Crouch Slide** (bug): Crouch and slide around. Walk then crouch.
- **Swim upwards**: When in water, **hold** the spacebar to swim upwards.

## How to get a 1down (the opposite of a 1up)
- **Fall**: Walk into the abyss
- **Lava**: Land on Lava enough times
